Privacy Policy

FHWebCheck does not collect, store, or share personal user data.

Information We Collect

The app stores website URLs entered by the user locally on the device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

FHWebCheck does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to alert users when a monitored website becomes unavailable.

Data Control

Users can delete all stored data by removing URLs within the app or uninstalling the app.

Contact

If you have questions, contact: support@fhwebcheck.com
