Common Questions

Why is my site showing as offline?

Temporary network issues or server downtime can cause a site to appear offline. Try checking again after a few minutes.

How often are sites checked?

The app checks site availability when opened and periodically in the background, depending on iOS system limitations.

Not receiving notifications?

Ensure notifications are enabled in your iPhone settings for FHWebCheck.

We typically respond within 1–2 business days.
